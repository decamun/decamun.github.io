Engineering is my passion. I pour myself into my work with gusto and creativity. I think outside the box, I don't just tinker. I try to make requirements-driven decisions and maintain a standard of excellence.

I am Devin, a mechanical engineer and designer

I grew up building Lego spaceships in my room, and in some (but not all) ways, not a lot has changed. Today I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Pennsylvania. I'm interested in robotics and space technologies. I love to learn, and I have plenty of experience in both the technical and practical aspects of engineering. Aside from what I've learned in my classes, I can design, machine and program in an applied workplace environment, and I have interned in aerospace tooling and robotics. Feel free to take a look at my blog to see current projects, or download my resume and portfolio.
